Vitality's Privacy Pledge
Vitality collects hydration, activity, and location data to provide personalized wellness insights and recommendations. We want you to know exactly how your data is used and who may have access to it.
Data SecurityWe collect hydration, activity, and location data that you record through Vitality+. All data is encrypted with enterprise‑grade protocols and stored on secure servers.
How We Use Your DataYour data helps us generate personalized health metrics, deliver tailored reminders, and improve the app’s features. You can view, update, or delete your data at any time via your dashboard.
Sharing & PrivacyWe never sell your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with research partners to improve health outcomes, but your identifiable information remains private. Private Mode keeps your activity completely anonymous.
For more information about our privacy features, visit our help center.
